Part 1: Understanding Dataset & Distributions

In cybersecurity, analyzing datasets can help identify patterns such as login attempts, suspicious IPs, and user behavior. A dataset is a structured collection of data, typically organized in rows (records) and columns (variables).

A variable is a characteristic or attribute measured in the dataset. Univariate analysis looks at one variable at a time, while bivariate analysis explores the relationship between two variables.
